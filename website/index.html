ICEland

Your personal command-line tool for focused digital areas, notes, and time tracking.

What is ICEland?

ICEland is a Rust-based CLI tool that helps you separate your digital life into focused areas such as work, learning, math, gaming, traveling, and trading. It organizes your notes, links, and logs your time automatically.

Who is this for?

  • Students wanting structured study and personal time.
  • Developers looking for clean workflows.
  • Anyone struggling with distractions and mixed files.

Core Features

  • Separate folders per area: ~/.iceland/<area>/
  • Notes, projects, analysis, flashcards per area
  • Separate browser profile spaces per area
  • Quick CLI or TUI area switching
  • Reset (“destroy”) notes or browser data per area
  • Basic time logging: ~/.iceland/sessions.csv

Installation

Build from source: 

cargo build --release

Or use prebuilt Linux binaries (see below). Place in your $PATH and run: iceland.

Commands

Initialize areas

iceland init

Switch areas (CLI)

iceland switch learning
iceland switch gaming

Switch areas with TUI

iceland tui

Add notes

iceland notes learning "Study Rust at 5pm"
iceland notes math "Do exercise 5"

Reset browser or notes

iceland destroy gaming browser
iceland destroy learning notes

Check time stats

iceland stats

Download Binaries

x86_64 Binary aarch64 Binary
